Exam Instructions

UGC NET Paper 2 · Anthropology · June 2025 PYQ · 100 Questions · 200 Marks · 120 Minutes

📌

General Instructions

  • The total duration of the examination is 120 minutes (2 Hours). The timer will auto-submit your test when time expires.
  • This paper contains 100 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs), each carrying 2 marks. Total marks: 200.
  • There is no negative marking for wrong answers. You are encouraged to attempt all questions.
  • Each question has four options (A, B, C, D). Select the most appropriate answer by clicking the option.
  • Use "Save & Next" to save your response and move to the next question. Only saved responses are considered final.
  • You may revisit and change your answers anytime before final submission.
  • Click "Submit Test" in the right panel only when you are sure you want to end the test.
🧭

Navigation & Actions

  • Save & Next: Saves your current answer and moves to the next question. Palette turns Green.
  • Mark for Review & Next: Flags the question for later review and moves forward. Palette turns Purple.
  • Clear Response: Removes your selected answer for the current question.
  • ← Prev / Next →: Navigate between questions freely without saving.
  • Pause ⏸: Pauses the countdown timer. Questions are hidden during pause to prevent cheating.
